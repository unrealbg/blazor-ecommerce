Brand manifesto

Luxury online is communicated through editing, pacing, and confidence.

The goal is not to show more products. The goal is to make every product feel deliberate. This concept favors stronger hierarchy, quieter accents, refined metadata, and an editorial cadence that reduces clutter while improving trust.

  • Fewer cards per row so product imagery and material details feel more premium.
  • Stronger section framing so campaigns, categories, and hero stories have distinct rhythm.
  • Trust signals that support conversion without drifting into marketplace clutter.

Interaction guidance

Keep the drawer brief, tactile, and conversion-minded.

  • Show only the essentials: line item, quantity, subtotal, and a direct path to checkout.
  • Preserve product imagery and material cues so the customer still feels the brand while reviewing the bag.
  • Add only one secondary action, usually "View bag", to avoid decision dilution.
